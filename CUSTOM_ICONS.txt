CUSTOM ICON QUICK GUIDE
=======================

Open Icon Studio inside Tower Optimizer to upload artwork without editing files.
Custom artwork is stored in data\custom_icons and survives normal upgrades.

Recommended: transparent PNG, 256x256 or 512x512.

Fixed icon examples:
  resources\coins.png
  resources\stones.png
  resources\gems.png
  ultimate_weapons\golden_tower.png
  ultimate_weapons\black_hole.png
  ultimate_weapons\death_wave.png
  systems\cards.png
  systems\modules.png
  systems\relics.png

Collection examples:
  relics\<relic-name>.png
  modules\black-hole-digestor.png
  cards\coins.png

Names are converted to lowercase hyphenated filenames automatically by Icon Studio.

PUBLIC REPOSITORY NOTE
----------------------
Runtime overrides under data\custom_icons are ignored by Git. Do not force-add exact game artwork unless you have redistribution permission.
